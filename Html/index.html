Collaborative E-challan

Since everything is on an electronic platform, the concept of e- challan system has been introduced.
A vehicle e-challan is a computer-generated challan used by the Traffic Police and is being issued to all the traffic
defaulters in Nepal. The Government of Nepal has initiated this process to make the traffic services convenient,
easy as well as transparent to the general public

E Challan

It is a coursework of our module Collaborative Development where we will be working as a team of four to complete the project on the topic E-Challan. E-Challan is a system that allows traffic to create and print challan in both pdf and excel. The functionality of the project is CRUD.
